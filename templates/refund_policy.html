Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

At Prashant IT Consultancy, we strive to provide high-quality consulting services. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent for all our clients.

When Refunds Are Allowed

We offer refunds in the following circumstances:

  • Consultant cancellation of scheduled session
  • Technical issues preventing service delivery
  • Double charges due to payment processing errors
  • Service not delivered as described

When Refunds Are Not Allowed

Refunds will not be provided in the following situations:

  • Client no-show without prior notice
  • Change of mind after service delivery
  • Late cancellation (within 24 hours of scheduled session)
  • Client-prepared materials not provided for consultation

Refund Processing Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method. Please note that some payment providers may take additional time to process the refund.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team with the following information:

  • Your booking reference number
  • Date and time of the consultation
  • Reason for refund request
  • Any supporting documentation
